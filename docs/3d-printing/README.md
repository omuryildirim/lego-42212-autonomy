# 3D Printing

Printable connector parts for integrating common hobby servos and small motors with Lego Technic axle pieces.

## Available adapters

- [EMax ES08MA II mini servo to Lego axle adapter](./emax-es08ma-ii/README.md)
- [SG90 mini servo to Lego axle adapter](./sg90/README.md)
- [GA12-N20 micro metal gear motor to Lego axle adapter](./ga12-n20/README.md)

## Notes

- These parts are intended to couple a motor or servo output shaft to Lego Technic axle-compatible pieces.
- The Lego axle side is designed around pieces such as Lego Technic, Axle 6L (#3706), with nominal 4.4 mm x 4.4 mm dimensions.
