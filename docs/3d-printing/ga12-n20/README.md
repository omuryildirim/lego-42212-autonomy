# GA12-N20 Micro Metal Gear Motor To Lego Axle Adapter

This piece is designed to connect a GA12-N20 micro metal gear motor to Lego pieces.

## Interface details

- Motor side: GA12-N20 shaft
- Motor shaft nominal diameter: 3.0 mm
- Typical shaft profile: D-shaft, narrowed to about 2.5 mm on the flat side
- Lego side: axle-compatible interface for pieces such as Lego Technic, Axle 6L (#3706)
- Lego axle nominal dimensions: 4.4 mm x 4.4 mm

## Part dimensions

- Overall length: 16 mm
- Engagement length per side: 8 mm
